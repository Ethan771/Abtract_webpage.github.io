About our band

We are Abstract band. We are a band from Dukekunshan university. Our band‘s name comes from a cyber word mostly used in China. We often use this word to show our incomprehension to someone’s behavior. But in another way, we can also be abstract if others don’t understand us. We think abstract shows our attitude towards life. We are all abstract. We play a wide range of music types. In the past we played pop music, ACG music, rock music, and R&B music. And we will try more music types in the future. We mainly have 5 members in our band. Qinyu Chen is our lead vocal. Binjie Shi is our drummer. Jiaqi Yao is our keyboard and vocal. Yiteng Zhang is our guitar and vocal. Yu Zhou is our bass.
